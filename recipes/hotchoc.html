Hot chocolate recipe

Amazing for a cold winter day, nothing better then sipping hot chocolate while covered in blankets and looking at the rain outside...

Indgredients:

  • 1.25 cups milk
  • 40 grams dark (about 70% cacao) chocolate
  • 1 tbsp cacao
  • 2 tbsp pure maple syrup
  • A splash of pure vanilla extract and a tiny amount of salt
  1. In a small saucepan, heat up the milk on medium-low heat
  2. Chop up the chocolate and add to the pan
  3. While stirring, add the cacao, maple syrup, vanilla extract and a bit of salt
  4. After everything melted, pour into a nice mug
  5. Optional: top with some whipped cream
  6. Enjoy!
