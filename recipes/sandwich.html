Cold cut meats sandwich recipe

This sandwich makes up much more then the sum of its parts, makes for an amazing and actually pretty healthy lunch

Indgredients:

  • Any type of bread, a braid challah or baguette is preferred.
  • 4 slices of cornbeef
  • 4 slices of pastrami
  • 6 slices of salami
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp mayo
  • Lettuce, tomato and some pickles
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Instructions

  1. Cut the bread into to slices and toast them in a toaster
  2. Heat up a pan with oil to medium-low
  3. Cook the egg sunny-side-up until just slightly undercooked
  4. Spread ketchup and mayo on both slices of bread, mix them to make a nice sauce
  5. Assemble all the meats with the egg on top, then put the lettuce, tomato and some pickles
  6. Assemble and enjoy!
