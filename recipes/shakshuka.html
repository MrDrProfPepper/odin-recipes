Italian style Shakshuka

Makes an amazing breakfast or dinner, also really healthy

For me there's nothing better for breakfast then a good shakshuka with tahini and toasted pita.

Indgredients:

  • 5 large eggs
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 1 medium onion
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 20 gr tomato paste
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 1 tsp sweet paprika
  • 20 gr feta cheese, shredded
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat up a wide pan with olive oil on medium heat
  2. Cut the onion into cubes
  3. Cook the onion in the pan until just sweated
  4. Add garlic and red pepper flakes and cook for 1 more minute
  5. Add tomato paste and cook for 1 more minute
  6. Add crushed tomatoes, along with spices, salt and black pepper
  7. Simmer on low heat until the sauce reduces and the tomatoes taste sweet
  8. Make 5 little divotes in the sauce and crack in the eggs
  9. Cover the pan and wait for the eggs to be just slightly undercooked
  10. Optional: add shredded cheese, cover and let steam for 2 more minutes
  11. Serve with some toasted pita and tahini on the side, and enjoy!
